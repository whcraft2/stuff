Try to take the ball to furthest point on a route consisting of cubes. There are 1 and 2 player modes in the game. You can buy several bonuses on "Shop" by using the collected diamonds. Bonuses include diamond magnet, 2x diamond counter, and armor for traps. You may use the bonuses purchased from the Shop section in order to progress further distances in single player game mode.

First one to fall loses the round in 2 player mode. Therefore, better be more careful than your friend if you are willing to win. As you progress on levels, game will get harder and there will be a loser!

Game Controls:  "A,D" and "Left - Right Arrows". 

Game Features:

  • Flat graphics, relaxing music, and casual gaming experience.
  • Single and multiplayer modes.
  • Realistic in-game physics and controls.
  • High score leaderboard.
